梦游

June 03, 2022 at Eugene, OR
梦游千里千重山 曲折百转百草连 浊酒三盏三生事 清风一抚一缕泉
 

A Dream (Translation)

I dreamt of a long hike in the mountains, The roads are twisted, and the ivies block the trail. I would laugh at these worldly matter with a glass of crappy wine, Because there will always be clear springs wherever the wind goes.
