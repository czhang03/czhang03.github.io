Research Info

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Boston University POPV Group working with Professor Marco Gaboardi.

In general, I am interested in applying abstract mathematical techniques in Algebra to practical problems in Program Semantics, Program Logics, and Verification. I generally enjoy Mathematics, Computer Science, and their intersections.

Specifically, I am interested in using algebraic technique to develop general, reusable, and modular logic for program verification, and exploring their specific use cases in the real world.

I am the organizer of BU POPV Seminar, and category theory reading group. I have previously organized a Coq reading group. Please contact me if you are interested giving a talk at any of these events.
